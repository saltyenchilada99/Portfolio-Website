Summer 2024 Internship

In the summer of 2024, I worked at H&K group, a construction company located in the northeast region. I teamed up with their block and supply division to design a physical and digital operator's manual for incoming new construction workers as a reference on how to safely and effectively handle the operating machines throughout the construction site. I designed the website with idea of keeping the layout simple and concise, to make the user-experience beginner-friendly to new-workers while maintaining the H&K design style of a heavy-duty construction aesthetic
